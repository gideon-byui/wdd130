BIG WHALES RAFTING

At Big Whales Rafting, our mission is to provide unforgettable and safe outdoor adventures that connect people with the beauty and power of nature through thrilling water rafting experiences. We will help you navigate rough waters with top-notch equipments and with the help and guides from highly trained experts and proffessionals to safely head down the rivers. We are dedicated to: Safety First: Ensuring the safety of our guests and staff is our top priority. We maintain the highest industry standards for equipment, training, and safety protocols to create an environment where everyone can enjoy their adventure with peace of mind. Environmental Stewardship: We are committed to preserving and protecting the pristine natural environments in which we operate. Our practices emphasize Leave No Trace principles, responsible waste management, and partnerships with local conservation organizations to ensure the places we love remain vibrant and untouched

History

Empowering Adventure Seekers to Conquer the Rapids, One Thrill at a Time has been the motive behing Big Whales Rafting since we started started operating in 1975, We offer rafting on the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers in Glenwood Springs, Since 1974, we have been family owned and operated, rafting the Shoshone section of Glenwood Canyon and beyond. Our journey started with a shared vision to introduce people to the exhilarating beauty and power of river adventures. With nothing more than a few rafts and unwavering determination, we set out to conquer the roaring rapids of the Colorado Fork Rivers, our first playground. We pioneered the concept of guided water rafting expeditions in the region, taking participants on unforgettable journeys through breathtaking canyons, lush forests, and magnificent waterways. Trips vary from mild and great for families, to trips exclusively for physically fit, experienced rafters. No matter what type of river adventure you are seeking, Whitewater Rafting, LLC can make it happen for you! We strive to make your raft trip the very best of your life.
