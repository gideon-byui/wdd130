Trips Category

Package Details

Beginner/Scenic Rafting
  • This category is ideal for individuals or groups with little to no previous rafting experience.
  • These trips typically take place on calmer sections of the river, offering a more relaxed and scenic experience.
  • This option is suitable for families, beginners, or those looking for a more leisurely river experience.
Intermediate/Adventure Rafting
  • Intermediate-level rafting trips are designed for individuals who have some prior rafting experience and are looking for a more exhilarating adventure.
  • These trips often include sections of the river with moderate rapids and obstacles.
  • Participants receive more extensive training and may need to actively paddle and navigate the rapids.
Advanced/Extreme Rafting
  • Advanced or extreme rafting trips are tailored to experienced and adventurous participants.
  • These trips take place on challenging sections of the river with intense rapids and fast-flowing water.
  • Participants are typically required to have previous rafting experience and strong paddling skills.
